# Investment Basics

## What is Startup Investing?

Startup investing involves providing capital to early-stage companies in exchange for equity ownership. This high-risk, high-reward activity can be both financially rewarding and personally fulfilling as you help new companies grow.

## Types of Startup Investments

- Direct equity investments
- Convertible notes
- SAFEs (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)
- Revenue-based financing

## Investment Stages

- Pre-seed
- Seed
- Series A
- Series B
- Series C and beyond

## Basic Terminology

- Cap table
- Dilution
- Pre-money vs. Post-money valuation
- Term sheet
